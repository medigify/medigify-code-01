Question: Which vitamin is known to help prevent respiratory tract infections:
Options:
a) Vitamin K
b) Vitamin B12
c) Vitamin B9
d) Vitamin D
Correct Option:
Ans. Vitamin D
Explanation: Vitamin D enhances innate immunity in the respiratory tract. It stimulates production of antimicrobial peptides such as cathelicidin in respiratory epithelium. These peptides help destroy bacteria and viruses entering the airways. Deficiency is associated with increased risk of respiratory infections.
