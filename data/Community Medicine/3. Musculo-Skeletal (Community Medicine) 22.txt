Question: What is the most common type of MSD:
Options:
a) Carpal tunnel syndrome
b) Tendinitis
c) Back pain
d) Tennis elbow
Correct Option:
Ans. Back Pain
Explanation: Low back pain is the most prevalent musculoskeletal disorder worldwide, especially due to poor posture and prolonged sitting. It is highly emphasized in occupational health. Other options are specific localized disorders, but back pain affects a larger population.

Question: What is text neck:

b) A type of MSD caused by excessive use of mobile devices
c) A type of MSD caused by poor posture
d) A type of MSD caused by repetitive motions
Correct Option:
Ans. A type of MSD caused by excessive use of mobile devices
