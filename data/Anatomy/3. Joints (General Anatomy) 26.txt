Question: Which of the following belongs to diarthrosis variety of joints:
Options:
a) Gomphosis
b) Synchondrosis
c) Synovial
d) Symphysis
Correct Option:
Ans. Synovial
Explanation: Synovial joints, or diarthroses, are freely movable joints. Examples include the shoulder, hip, and knee. These joints allow movement in one or more axes and are the most mobile type of joint in the body.

Question: The synovial joints:
Options:
a) have synovial membranes covering articular surface of bones
b) Have hyaline cartilage covering the articular surface of bone
c) Are capsules made u p o f skeletal muscle fibers
d) Contain synovial fluid secreted by fibrous capsule of joint.
Correct Option:
Ans. Have hyaline cartilage covering the articular surface of bone
Explanation: The articular surfaces of synovial joints are covered by hyaline cartilage, which reduces friction and absorbs mechanical shock. The synovial fluid in the joint cavity lubricates the surfaces, but it is secreted by the synovial membrane, not the fibrous capsule.
