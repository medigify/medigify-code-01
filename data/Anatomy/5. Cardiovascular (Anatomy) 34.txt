Question: Dominance of coronary arterial system being right or left depend upon which of the following structure:
Options:
a) Circumflex artery
b) Posterior interventricular artery
c) SA node
d) Anterio interventricular artery
Correct Option:
Ans. Posterior interventricular artery
Explanation: Coronary dominance depends on which coronary artery gives rise to the posterior interventricular (descending) artery (PDA).
Right coronary artery → Right dominant (most common 67%)
Circumflex artery → Left dominant (18%)
Both → 15%
Therefore, dominance is determined by PDA origin.
