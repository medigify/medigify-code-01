Ans. Arcuate artery
Explanation: The Arcuate artery arises from the Dorsalis Pedis Artery as it crosses the tarsal bones. It runs laterally across the bases of the metatarsals and gives off the dorsal metatarsal arteries. A penetrating injury in the webspace between the first and second toes is highly likely to sever the arcuate artery or the first dorsal metatarsal artery, leading to profuse bleeding.
