Question: The autonomic nervous system:
Options:
a) Control Skeletal muscles
b) Arise from spinal cord only
c) Prepare the body for fight & flight response
d) has no connection with adrenal medulla
Correct Option:
Ans. Prepare the body for fight and flight response
Explanation: The autonomic nervous system controls involuntary activities. Its sympathetic division prepares the body for fight or flight, increasing heart rate, dilating pupils, and redirecting blood flow, unlike skeletal muscles which are voluntary.

Question: Which of the following is not a component of CNS:
Options:
a) Neuron
b) Spinal cord
c) Basal ganglia
d) Cranial nerve
Correct Option:
Ans. Cranial nerve
Explanation: CNS includes the brain, spinal cord, neurons, and basal ganglia. Cranial nerves belong to the peripheral nervous system; they carry signals to and from CNS but are not part of it.

Question: Outermost Covering of nerve is called:
Options:
a) Endoneurium
b) Perineurium
c) Epineurium
d) Epimysium
Correct Option:
Ans. Epineurium
Explanation: The epineurium is the outermost connective tissue covering a whole nerve. It provides mechanical protection, houses blood vessels, and surrounds multiple fascicles of nerve fibers.
