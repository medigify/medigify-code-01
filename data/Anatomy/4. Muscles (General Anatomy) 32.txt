Question: Regarding Muscle all of the above statements are correct except:
Options:
a) Smooth muscles are spindle shaped
b) Cardiac muscles are striated
c) Skeletal muscles are in voluntary control
d) Smooth muscles of ciliary body are voluntary
Correct Option:
Ans. Smooth muscles of ciliary body are voluntary
Explanation: Smooth muscles, including those in the ciliary body, are involuntary. They are spindle-shaped, while cardiac muscles are striated and skeletal muscles are under voluntary control.

Question: Epimysium Covers:
Options:
a) Muscle fiber
b) Bundle of muscle fibers
c) Whole muscle
d) Ligament
Correct Option:
Ans. Whole muscle
Explanation: The epimysium is a connective tissue sheath that surrounds the entire muscle, providing protection and transmitting force to tendons. Fascicles and individual fibers have separate sheaths: perimysium and endomysium.

Question: Which type of muscle fiber arrangement produces the greatest power due to a larger number of fibers per unit area:
Options:
a) Parallel
b) Fusiform
c) Pennate
d) Circular
Correct Option:
Ans. Pennate
Explanation: Pennate muscles have fibers arranged obliquely to the tendon, allowing a larger number of fibers per unit area. This arrangement maximizes force production, unlike parallel or circular muscles, which favor range of movement over power.

Question: The functional unit of skeletal muscle responsible for contraction is:
Options:
a) Myofibril
b) Sarcomere
c) Sarcolemma
d) Sarcoplasm
Correct Option:
Ans. Sarcomere
Explanation: The sarcomere is the structural and functional unit of skeletal muscle responsible for contraction. It is composed of actin (thin) and myosin (thick) filaments, whose sliding generates muscle shortening.
