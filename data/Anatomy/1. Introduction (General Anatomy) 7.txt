Question: In anatomical position:
Options:
a) The body is lying down position with face directed upward
b) The body is erect with palm directed backward
c) The body erect, with arms on side, palms facing forward
d) Hips & Knees are fully extended
Correct Option:
Ans. The body erect, with arms on side, palms facing forward
Explanation: In this position the body is regarded as standing erect, eyes looking toward horizon, upper limbs hanging by side with the palms of hands directed forward and feet are together with toes pointing forward.

Question: Which body plane divides body in equal 2 right & left halves:
Options:
a) Transverse
b) Coronal
c) Sagittal
d) Parasagittal
Correct Option:
Ans. Sagittal
Explanation: Any plane parallel to the median plane is called a sagittal plane.

Question: Rotates the radius medially so that the palm of the hand faces posteriorly and its dorsum faces anteriorly:
Options:
a) Pronation
b) Supination
c) Protraction
d) Retraction
Correct Option:
Ans. Pronation
Explanation: Pronation turns the dorsum of the hand forward without the rotation of the upper arm.
