Question: Which of the following statements is NOT true for glycogen:

d) Deposits in large amounts in inherited disorders
e) Hydrolysed by alpha-amylase
Correct Option:
Ans. Gives blue color with iodine solution
Explanation: Starch gives a blue color with iodine, whereas glycogen gives a reddish-brown color. Glycogen gives a reddish-brown color with iodine because it is highly branched, producing short helices.
