Question: The respiratory system is an outgrowth of what:
Options:
a) Middle mediastinum
b) Ventral wall of foregut
c) Anterior abdominal wall
d) Aortic arches
Correct Option:
Ans. Ventral wall of foregut
Explanation: The respiratory system develops as a diverticulum from the ventral wall of the primitive foregut, known as the laryngotracheal diverticulum.
