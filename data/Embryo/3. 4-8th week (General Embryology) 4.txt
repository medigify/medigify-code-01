Question: When does Neurulation completes:
Options:
a) By the end of 3rd week
b) By the end of 4th week
c) By the end of 5th week
d) By the end of 6th week
Correct Option:
Ans. By the end of 4th week
Explanation: Neurulation is the process whereby the neural plate forms the neural tube. Neurulation completion is demarcated by closure of the neuropores. Cranial neuropore occurs at by day 25 at 18-20 somite stage and the caudal neuropore closes at day 28 at the 25 somite stage, marking the completion of neurulation
