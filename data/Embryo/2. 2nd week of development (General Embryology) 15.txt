Question: A 20-year-old woman presents with severe right-sided abdominal pain and signs of internal bleeding. She has been sexually active without contraception and missed her last menstrual period. Which disorder must be considered in the diagnosis:
Options:
a) Preeclampsia
b) Appendicitis
c) Normal pregnancy
d) Ectopic tubal pregnancy
Correct Option:
Ans: Ectopic tubal pregnancy
Explanation: Ectopic pregnancy, especially in the fallopian tube, presents with abdominal pain, missed periods, and internal bleeding. Prompt diagnosis is critical to prevent complications.

Question: Which component plays the most active role in invading the endometrium during blastocyst implantation:
Options:
a) Epiblast
b) Syncytiotrophoblast
c) Extraembryonic somatic mesoderm
d) Extraembryonic visceral mesoderm
Correct Option:
Ans: Syncytiotrophoblast
