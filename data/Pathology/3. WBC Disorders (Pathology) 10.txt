Question: Acute lymphatic leukemia is predominantly a disease of:
Options:
a) The elderly patients
b) Females
c) Adult males
d) Children
Correct Option:
Ans. Children
Explanation: ALL is the most common leukemia in children. It involves malignant proliferation of lymphoid precursors (lymphoblasts). Presents with anemia, infections, and bleeding.

Question: The most common and ultimately lethal extramedullary complication of multiple myeloma is:
Options:
a) Amyloidosis of the brain
b) Amyloidosis of the liver
c) Kidney disease
d) Peritonitis
Correct Option:
Ans. Kidney disease
Explanation: Multiple myeloma commonly causes renal failure. Light chain deposition damages kidneys (myeloma kidney). This is a major cause of death.

Question: Auer rods are characteristic finding of:
Options:
a) ALL
b) CLL
c) AML
d) Burkitt lymphoma
Correct Option:
Ans. AML
Explanation: Auer rods are needle-like cytoplasmic inclusions. They are seen in acute myeloid leukemia. They are diagnostic of myeloid lineage blasts.

Question: The diagnosis of acute leukemia is based on:
Options:
a) Presence of Philadelphia chromosome
b) Presence of lymphadenopathy
c) History of fever and weakness
d) Presence of >30% blast cells in the bone marrow
Correct Option:
Ans. Presence of >30% blast cells in the bone marrow
Explanation: Acute leukemia is diagnosed when blasts exceed 20–30%. These immature cells replace normal marrow. Leads to pancytopenia.

Question: Which of the following is not consistent with chronic myeloid leukemia (CML):
Options:
a) Presence of Philadelphia chromosome
b) Presence of BCR-ABL gene product
c) Increased basophils
d) Thrombocytopenia
Correct Option:
Ans. Thrombocytopenia
Explanation: CML usually shows leukocytosis with thrombocytosis, not thrombocytopenia. Philadelphia chromosome and BCR-ABL fusion are characteristic features.

Question: Leukocyte alkaline phosphatase (LAP) score increased in:
Options:
a) CLL
b) CML
c) Leukemoid reaction
d) AML
Correct Option:
Ans. Leukemoid reaction
Explanation: LAP score is increased in reactive leukocytosis. It is decreased in CML. Helps differentiate CML from leukemoid reaction.
