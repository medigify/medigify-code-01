Question: A six year old boy develops gross hematuria after taking antimalarial drug, most probably the cause is:
Options:
a) G6PD deficiency
b) Immune hemolysis
c) Malaria
d) Paroxysmal nocturnal hemoglobinuria
Correct Option:
Ans. G6PD deficiency
Explanation: G6PD deficiency leads to decreased protection against oxidative stress. Antimalarial drugs (like primaquine) cause oxidative damage → RBC hemolysis. This results in hemoglobinuria (dark urine). It is an X-linked disorder.
